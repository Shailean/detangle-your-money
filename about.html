Detangle Your Money was created to address the unique financial circumstances that are faced by low or middle income people of color.

We partner and collaborate with community stakeholders to provide financial coaching and financial education workshops. We research the primary concerns of our target audience and tailor our financial content to be user-friendly.

We provide financial workshops and individual financial coaching to empower people to take control of their financial lives. We provide guidance in selecting the right financial products that will enable the proper management of credit and debt, budget creation, and asset building.

Detangle Your Money focuses on helping individuals become confident consumers in comparing financial products, maintaining low debt, taking advantage of savings opportunities, and outlining plans to reach their financial goals
